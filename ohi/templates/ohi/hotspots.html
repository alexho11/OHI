Hotspots

Our platform leverages Advanced Identification System (AIS) data to monitor and visualize fishing activities across the Gulf of Mexico and adjacent waters, with a focus on understanding their impact on ocean health. This interactive map highlights “hotspots” where fishing activities are detected, providing invaluable insights for maritime management, conservation efforts, and sustainable fishing practices.
